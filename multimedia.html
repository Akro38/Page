Outstanding programmers

Timothy John Berners-Lee | HTML

John "Tim" Berners-Lee (born June 8, 1955, London) is a British computer scientist, best known as the creator of URIs, URLs, HTTP, HTML, and the World Wide Web.

Guido van Rossum | Python

Guido (born January 31, 1956, in The Hague, Netherlands) is a Dutch programmer, best known as the creator of the Python programming language. Among Python developers, Guido is known as the project's "benevolent dictator for life," meaning that he continues to oversee Python's development process, making final decisions when necessary.

Brendan Eich | JavaScript

Brendan Eich (born July 4, 1961, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA) is an American programmer and the creator of the JavaScript programming language. He worked at Netscape since 1995, participated in the founding of Mozilla, and served as CTO of Mozilla Corporation until 2014.

Bjarne Stroustrup | C++

Bjarne Stroustrup (born December 30, 1950, Aarhus, Denmark) is a Danish programmer and the author of the C++ programming language.
